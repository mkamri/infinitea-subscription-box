About Us

About Us

We’re a small team of tea-lovers located in California, USA. We hand-dry our teas and add organic fruits, herbs, and spices to create our signature blends just for you.

Our Values

Always fair-trade and organic, we ship all of our teas in 100% post-consumer biodegradable packages. We never use synthetic dyes or fragrances, so you know you can trust your tea to be clean and healthy.
